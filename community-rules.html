Community Rules

Effective date: 2026-02-24

These rules apply to the Smart Investment Tips community (Trading Journal and Free Board) to keep discussions safe and useful.

1. Prohibited Content

  • Personal advertising or promotion (paid channels, solicitation, paid groups, referral recruitment)
  • Contact-harvesting or off-platform redirection (phone numbers, messenger IDs, external invite links)
  • Sexual, explicit, or age-inappropriate language/content
  • Harassment, threats, hate, slurs, or abusive language
  • Illegal activity promotion (drugs, illegal gambling, account trading), scams, or misleading profit claims
  • Spam, flooding, repeated characters, or excessive external links

2. Enforcement

  • Minor violations: warning or post restriction
  • Repeated/serious violations: temporary or extended posting ban
  • Potential legal violations may be escalated according to applicable procedures
Violations may lead to content removal, visibility restriction, or account-level posting bans without prior notice.

3. Reporting

If you find abusive or policy-violating posts, report them through the contact page.
