Technical Analysis for Beginners

8 min read · Feb 24, 2026

How Should You Use Indicators?

Indicators are not decision buttons; they are probability filters. Read price action and trend first, then use indicators for confirmation.

Three Recommendations for Beginners

  • Moving Average (MA): Identify trend direction (up/down)
  • RSI: Check overheating in overbought/oversold zones
  • MACD: Detect momentum shift hints

Practical Workflow Example

  • Step 1: Check whether price is above MA
  • Step 2: Check whether RSI indicates overheating
  • Step 3: Check for MACD shift signals
  • Step 4: Write stop and target first, then enter

Caution

Stacking too many similar indicators often adds noise and false confidence. For beginners, "one trend tool + one momentum tool + risk rules" is usually enough.
